Baghbaan Summer Internship Programme (BSIP)

BSIP is TCF’s volunteer initiative for youth aged 15-25 who want to transform lives through advocacy and action. Pakistan has the world's second-highest number of out-of-school children. As a Baghbaan Intern, you'll raise awareness about this education crisis and raise funds to sponsor children's education in TCF school campuses.

Duration

July - August 2026

Commitment

8-10 Hours Per Week

Format

Hybrid (Events & Virtual)

Note: This is not a teaching or career counseling role. Baghbaan Ambassadors focus purely on advocacy, awareness, and fundraising to support students.
